Our Story

Your neighbourhood coffee shop in Wapping

We're a small, independent coffee shop right here in Wapping, serving the Aldgate and East London community. Started by locals, for locals.

Every morning, we brew fresh coffee from our friends at Limini Coffee. Whether you're from Aldgate East, Tower Hill, or Whitechapel, we know your name, remember your order, and genuinely look forward to seeing you. Come grab a coffee, have a chat, and feel right at home.
