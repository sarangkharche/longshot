Our Story

Born from a love of great coffee

We started Longshot because we couldn't find the coffee we wanted in our neighbourhood. So we created it ourselves.

Every morning, we hand-select beans from our friends at Limini Coffee and brew them with care. No fuss, no fancy talk—just really good coffee made by people who actually care about what's in your cup.

The Experience

More than just coffee

Come for the coffee, stay for the vibe. Whether you're catching up with a friend, powering through work, or just need a moment to yourself—we've got you.

Our baristas know their stuff and love talking coffee (try us on brew methods, we dare you). But they also know when you just need that perfect flat white without the chat.
